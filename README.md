📊 Amazon Sales Data Analysis Dashboard (Power BI)



This project presents an interactive Amazon Sales Analysis Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI. The dashboard helps analyze sales performance, product trends, and customer engagement through dynamic visualizations and KPIs.







🚀 Key Features

📈 Sales Performance Analysis – Track YTD Sales ($2.18M) and QTD Sales ($811K) to monitor business growth.

📦 Product Insights – Analyze 27.75K products sold and identify top-performing product categories.

⭐ Customer Engagement – Monitor 19.42M reviews to understand product popularity and customer feedback.







📊 Dashboard Insights

📅 Monthly Sales Trends – Visualizes sales growth across the year.

📊 Weekly Sales Analysis – Tracks sales fluctuations week by week.

🛍 Category-wise Sales – Compares sales performance across different product categories.

🏆 Top 5 Products by Sales – Identifies the best-selling products.

⭐ Top 5 Products by Reviews – Highlights products with the highest customer engagement.








🛠 Tools & Technologies

📊 Microsoft Power BI

🔄 Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

📐 DAX (KPIs & Calculations)

📁 Sales Dataset






🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to provide data-driven insights into Amazon product sales, helping businesses
identify sales trends, top products, and customer engagement patterns for better decision-making.
